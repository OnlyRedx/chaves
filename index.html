About

Elena is a multidisciplinary designer with 8+ years of experience across brand identity, product design, and digital art direction. Rooted in minimalism, her work values precision, atmosphere, and purposeful detail.

She has collaborated with studios and independent creators worldwide — from early-stage startups to cultural institutions. Currently based between Lisbon and Berlin, working remotely and with selected clients.

⚡️ Approach: research-led, human-centered, and quietly bold.
